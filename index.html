how to break up with a browser tab
QUEENIE WU, DESKTOP PERFORMANCE

click to progress through breakup





Hoarding browser tabs isn’t novel. But when I try to clean them out every so often, I find myself more attached to them than I should logically be.
I’m convinced that by keeping them open, they would remind me of open to-dos, to spark on inspiration, or to finally write that residency application.
Closing a great link feels like saying goodbye to something special.

I wonder, what if it weighs as heavily on the tabs themselves as it does on me?
